# Write a unit practice

Read [the unit-practice standard](references/unit-practice-standard.md).

Produce:

- five to ten new questions from every section in the unit;
- topic-labelled review followed by mixed practice without method cues;
- conceptual, procedural, representational, and applied work;
- directions and workspace appropriate to each task;
- a unit self-assessment; and
- one short and one fully explained answer for every numbered item.

Do not copy section problem sets or reuse them with cosmetic value changes. Reject a practice that
omits an objective, contains fewer than 20 percent mixed questions, or lacks enough information for
the student to choose a method independently.
