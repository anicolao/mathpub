# Write a self-assessment

Read [the self-assessment standard](references/self-assessment-standard.md).

Produce a short reflection addressed directly to the student. Ask the learner to:

- rate confidence using a consistent scale;
- cite completed work as evidence;
- identify and correct a specific misconception;
- explain one method in their own words; and
- choose a concrete next action.

For a unit, include every section and help the student prioritize review. Never treat confidence
alone as proof of mastery, and never discuss assessment design or what the author intended.
