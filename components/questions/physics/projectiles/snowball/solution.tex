Take upward as positive. The velocity components at the roof edge are
\[
  v_x=v_0\cos\theta,
  \qquad
  v_y=-v_0\sin\theta.
\]
At impact the vertical position is zero, so
\[
  0=h+v_y t-\frac12gt^2.
\]
The positive root gives $t=\mpvalue{impact_time}$. Horizontal motion is uniform; therefore
\[
  x=v_xt=\boxed{\mpvalue{impact_distance}}.
\]
