A snowball rolls from a barn roof whose surface slopes downward at \mpvalue{roof_angle}. At the
roof edge, \mpvalue{barn_height} above level ground, its speed is \mpvalue{launch_speed}. Ignore
air resistance. How far horizontally from the roof edge does it strike the ground? Round to one
decimal place.

\begin{center}
\begin{tikzpicture}[x=1cm,y=1cm]
  \coordinate (E) at (0,\mpvalue{edge_height});
  \draw[very thick] (-2,0) -- (\mpvalue{impact_x}+0.55,0);
  \draw[very thick] (-2,0) -- (-2,{\mpvalue{edge_height}+\mpvalue{roof_dy}})
    -- (E);
  \draw[dashed] (E) -- (0,0);
  \draw[<->] (-0.25,0) -- node[left] {\mpvalue{barn_height}}
    (-0.25,\mpvalue{edge_height});
  \draw[thick,->] (E) --
    ++(\mpvalue{velocity_dx},\mpvalue{velocity_dy})
    node[right] {$v_0=\mpvalue{launch_speed}$};
  \draw (0.72,\mpvalue{edge_height})
    arc[start angle=0,end angle=-\mpvalue{roof_angle_number},radius=0.72];
  \node at ({0.95*cos(\mpvalue{roof_angle_number}/2)},
    {\mpvalue{edge_height}-0.95*sin(\mpvalue{roof_angle_number}/2)})

\end{tikzpicture}
