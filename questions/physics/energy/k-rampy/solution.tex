The vertical drop shown by the scale drawing is
\[
  h=L\sin\theta=(\mpvalue{length})\sin(\mpvalue{angle})=\mpvalue{height}.
\]
Conservation of mechanical energy gives
\[
  mg h=\frac12mv^2,
  \qquad
  v=\sqrt{2gL\sin\theta}=\boxed{\mpvalue{speed}}.
\]
The validation record checks the energy equation, the drawn ramp length, and the drawn angle from
the generated Cartesian coordinates.
